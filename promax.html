What's Inside

01

Ion Exchange Media

Premium resin softens water by exchanging hard minerals like calcium and magnesium for sodium ions, preventing scale buildup.

02

Catalytic Coconut Shell Carbon

NSF certified catalytic carbon with high microporosity removes chlorine, chloramines, taste, and odor for crisp, clean water.

03

Mid Vortech Plate

Proprietary Vortech distribution plate ensures optimal water flow and even distribution through the media bed for maximum contact time.

04

KDF 55

High-purity copper-zinc alloy reduces heavy metals, controls bacteria and algae growth, and extends the life of carbon media.

05

Bottom Vortech

Lower Vortech distribution system enables superior backwashing at lower flow rates, saving water and extending system life.
