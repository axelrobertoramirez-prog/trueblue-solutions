4-Stage Whole Home Filtration

TrueBlue Essentials

Our entry-level whole home water conditioning system. Four powerful filtration stages deliver clean, soft water to every tap in your home.

4 Filtration Stages
48K Grain Capacity
NSF Certified Components

What's Inside

01

Ion Exchange Media

Premium resin softens water by exchanging hard minerals like calcium and magnesium for sodium ions, preventing scale buildup.

02

Catalytic Coconut Shell Carbon

NSF certified catalytic carbon with high microporosity removes chlorine, chloramines, taste, and odor for crisp, clean water.

03

KDF 55

High-purity copper-zinc alloy reduces heavy metals, controls bacteria and algae growth, and extends the life of carbon media.

04

Garnet Filtration

High-density garnet media traps fine sediment and particles, delivering crystal-clear water throughout your home.

Includes Brine Tank

Complete system comes with a high-capacity brine tank for automatic regeneration cycles.
